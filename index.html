Cognitive Behavioral Therapy for Insomnia (CBTI)

The recommended first line of treatment for insomnia with lasting result and minimal side effects

CBTI is a systematic, research-supported treatment method that tackles the underlying root causes of insomnia. It integrates a multi-factorial approach towards improving sleep, such as sleep restriction therapy, cognitive restructuring, dealing with sleep anxiety, stimulus control...etc.

Research demonstrates that CBTI is highly successful in addressing insomnia, frequently leading to sustained enhancements in both the quality and duration of sleep. Studies indicate that CBTI's long-term effectiveness rivals or surpasses that of medication, while avoiding the potential drawbacks of dependency and tolerance linked to sleep medications.

A significant benefit of CBTI is its limited side effects in contrast to pharmaceutical treatments. While some individuals may encounter temporary sleep disturbances initially as they adapt to new sleep routines, these effects are generally short-lived and offset by the long-term advantages of enhanced sleep quality and duration.
